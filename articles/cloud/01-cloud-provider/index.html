Cloud platforms are the foundation of modern infrastructure. Understanding the major providers’ core services, pricing models, and architectural patterns is essential for any cloud engineer.

Why Multi-Cloud Knowledge Matters

Most organizations use multiple cloud providers or may switch providers. Understanding the fundamentals across platforms helps you:

  • Make informed architectural decisions
  • Avoid vendor lock-in where appropriate
  • Leverage best-of-breed services
  • Support multi-cloud and hybrid strategies
