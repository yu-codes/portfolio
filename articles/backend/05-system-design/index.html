System design is where backend engineering becomes an art. It’s about making architectural decisions that enable your system to scale, remain maintainable, and evolve with changing requirements.

Why System Design Matters

System design skills determine whether your architecture will:

  • Scale to millions of users without rewrites
  • Remain maintainable as the team grows
  • Handle failures gracefully
  • Support new features without major refactoring
